Health Library & Tips

Explore our comprehensive library of actionable advice to help manage and prevent diabetes through daily habits.

Diet & Nutrition

Managing the Glycemic Index

Focus on low-GI foods like whole oats, beans, and non-starchy vegetables. These foods break down slowly, keeping blood sugar levels stable throughout the day.

Physical Activity

Cardio vs. Resistance Training

A combination of both is ideal! Cardio improves cardiovascular health, while resistance training builds muscle mass which naturally increases your body's glucose uptake.

Rest & Recovery

The Importance of Sleep

Poor sleep can disrupt your hormones, leading to increased insulin resistance and cravings for high-sugar foods. Aim for 7-8 hours of uninterrupted sleep every night.

Mental Health

Stress Management

Chronic stress releases cortisol and adrenaline, which elevate blood sugar levels. Try incorporating 10 minutes of daily meditation or deep breathing exercises.

About the Project

Privacy & Security

Your health data is highly sensitive. We have engineered DiaPredict to be completely stateless. None of the data you input is saved to a database or tracked. It is processed in memory for inference and immediately discarded.
